# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Pokémon fans (casual players through nuzlockers) naming a Pokémon they just caught. They arrive from a share link or a search, generate a handful of names in under a minute, copy or share the result, and leave. Mostly mobile and casual desktop use.

## Product Purpose

A free, fast AI nickname generator: pick a Pokémon, optionally a theme, get five good names, share them. Success is a user finding a name they actually use in-game and sharing the link.

## Brand Personality

Playful, nostalgic, polished. It should feel like a well-made fan tool — Pokémon warmth without pretending to be an official product. Fun moments (confetti, themes) sit on top of a clean, trustworthy interface.

## Anti-references

- Corporate SaaS gloss (gradient heroes, metric cards, purple-blue gradients)
- Rainbow "gamer" UI — color everywhere, neon on black
- Official-looking Pokémon branding (no franchise logos or trade dress)

## Design Principles

1. The Pokémon is the hero — sprites and names get visual priority over chrome.
2. One brand red, used with intent — actions, identity, and highlights; never decoration everywhere.
3. Fast and legible on a phone first.
4. Delight in moments (result reveal, themes), calm everywhere else.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: 4.5:1 body text, 3:1 large text/UI. Works in light and dark themes. Respect prefers-reduced-motion for the confetti/entrance animations.
